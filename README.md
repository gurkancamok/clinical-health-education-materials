# Digital Clinical Education Guides

This repository contains clinically developed educational materials designed as digital health resources to support patient education and safe healthcare practices.

The materials translate complex medical information into clear, accessible, and reusable educational guides suitable for digital and clinical environments.

---

## Purpose

These guides were created to:
- Improve patient understanding and engagement
- Support standardized clinical education
- Enhance treatment adherence and safety
- Serve as reusable digital health education assets

They reflect real-world clinical knowledge transformed into structured educational products.
